HIV/AIDS Information

What is HIV?

HIV attacks the immune system and weakens the body.

What is AIDS?

AIDS is the advanced stage of HIV infection.

How HIV is transmitted

Through unprotected sex, blood, and mother to child.

Prevention

Use condoms, avoid sharing sharp objects, test regularly.
